About

GradWIC (Graduate Women in Computing) at UCSD is dedicated to fostering diversity and inclusion in Computer Science and STEM fields at UCSD and beyond. We are a diverse organization of graduate students in the CSE department who are committed to understanding the challenges facing women in STEM, and addressing those challenges with a combination of outreach, mentorship, and a strong support system. We put on a variety of events each quarter, from lunches with faculty to panel discussions to movie outings. Membership is not limited to women; all graduate students in CSE and related disciplines who are interested in supporting our mission of increasing diversity and equity in computing are welcome.
